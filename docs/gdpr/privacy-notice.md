# Privacy notice — Risk Register (internal)

*Draft for review. Provided to employees who use the Risk Register.*

**What we process.** When you sign in, the application records your directory id,
name and email (from your Microsoft account) and the actions you take in the
register (creating or changing risks, treatments and control mappings).

**Why.** To operate the organisation's risk-management process — attributing
changes to a person, notifying risk owners and stakeholders, and keeping an
accountable audit trail.

**Lawful basis.** Our legitimate interest in managing organisational risk; and,
for the audit trail, our legal obligation to keep accountable records.

**Who sees it.** Authorised colleagues within the risk-management function,
according to their role. Email notifications are sent via Microsoft 365. We do
not sell your data or use it for profiling or automated decisions.

**How long.** Your directory record is kept while you are an active user;
notifications are purged on a schedule; audit records are retained for
accountability. See the retention schedule.

**Your rights.** You can ask for a copy of your data (access/portability), or
ask us to erase it. On erasure we remove your name and email; entries in the
append-only audit trail are retained under our legal obligation, keyed only to a
pseudonymous identifier.

**Contact.** Reach the Data Protection Officer via the internal GRC channel.
